# OHS
OHS: Ontology of Human Suicide

OHS was initiated in 2015 when Dr. Xiaofu Pan and Dr. Shunzhou Deng were visiting scholars in the University of Michigan and working with Dr. Oliver He there. The work was idled for many years. 

The He laboratory has resumed the research in 2025.
